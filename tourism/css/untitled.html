Experience Kerala

Native ExperienceResponsible Tourism packages are offered in such a way that travellers coming to Kerala leave with the warmth and compassion of the village life of God's Own Country embedded deep in their hearts.
Idiyappam & Egg Roast
Most Keralites swear that the Idiappam and Egg Roast combination can go up against any breakfast cuisine from anywhere else on the planet.
Monsoon @ MunnarMarked by vast expanses of tea plantations, colonial bungalows, rivulets, waterfalls and cool weather, Munnar is also an ideal destination to enjoy the Monsoon rains
KathakaliFamous around the world, Kathakali's magnificence has won great admiration for the state of Kerala.
DharaIn Dhara, an Ayurvedic Therapeutic Programme, herbal oils, medicated milk or butter milk and decoctions are poured on the forehead/whole body in a special manner.
Champakkulam Boat RaceChampakkulam Boat Race, held in River Pamba, is the oldest Snake Boat Race in the State and kicks off the boat race season annually.
ChristianityChristianity is the third-most practised religion in Kerala, accounting for 18% of the population according to the Indian census. Although a minority, the Christian population of Kerala is proportionally much larger than that of India as a whole. A significant portion of the Indian Christian population resides in the state.
PadayaniAs the night winds blow, a splash of feral colours and daunting masks that wiggle to mysterious rhythms create a blazing spectacle that is unique to Padayani.
